1. The ₹50,000 Milestone (Host's Contribution)
​Jab koi host aapke platform par ₹50,000 ki kamayi (Income) poori kar lega, tabhi wo apna ek special "Charity Video" prachar ke liye banayega:
​100% Revenue Lock: Us video se hone wali poori kamayi (100%) host ko nahi milegi, balki seedha Cancer, Orphan (Anath), aur gareeb bacchon ki bimari ke ilaaj mein jayegi.
​Niyat Check: Ye host ki taraf se ek shukrana hoga ki usne platform se itna kamaya, aur ab wo dunya ki madad kar raha hai.
​2. The ₹15 Model (Direct Patient Support)
​Aapne jo ₹15 ka model banaya hai, wo seedha logon ki jaan bachane ke kaam aayega:
​₹10 Maintenance: Ye system ko chalane aur service ke liye hoga [cite: 2026-01-10].
​₹5 Direct to Patient: Har transaction se ye ₹5 seedha mareez ke paas jayenge [cite: 2026-01-10].
​Company's Own Money: Sabse badi baat yeh hai ki ye paisa hum kisi se maang nahi rahe hain, balki 2% charity income aur company ke apne profit se de rahe hain.
​3. No Donations, Only Earnings
​Sultanat ka kanoon saaf hai:
​Hum dunya se donation (chanda) nahi maangenge.
